Burrito Recipe

Description

A burrito is a dish in Mexican and Tex-Mex cuisine that took form in California cuisine, consisting of a flour tortilla wrapped into a sealed cylindrical shape around various ingredients.

I like burritos!

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • White Rice
  • Beans
  • Chicken or Beef
  • Tomatillo Salsa
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Pico de Gaio
  • Sauces

Steps

  1. Heat tortilla
  2. Add white rice
  3. Add beans
  4. Add meats
  5. Add salsa and pico
  6. Add cheese and sauces
  7. Wrap burrito
  8. Done!
