HSP Recipe

Description

HSP is a popular middle-eastern food and is delicious.

The dish is made up of choices of proteins, sauces, onions, cheese and fries!

Ingredients

  • Fries
  • Lamb or Chicken or Both
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Garlic Sauce
  • Barbeque Sauce
  • Chilli Sauce

Steps

  1. Fry the chips and onions
  2. Cook meats
  3. Pour chips into a box or container
  4. Place lamb or chicken on top of fries
  5. Add onions and cheese on top
  6. Finally, drizzle the trinity sauce over everything
